Call for Papers - Research Demo Session (RDS)


Call for RDS PDF: Download PDF

The IEEE International Symposium on Smart Electronic Systems (IEEE iSES 2025) invites authors to submit proposals to the Research Demo Session (RDS). This session provides a platform for researchers to showcase live demonstrations and hardware prototypes of their papers and projects in the following domains:
  • Nanoelectronic, VLSI and Sensor Systems (NVS)
  • Energy-Efficient, Reliable VLSI Systems (ERS)
  • Hardware/Software for the Internet of smart Things and Healthcare (IoTH)
  • Hardware for Secure Information Processing (SIP)
  • Hardware/Software Solutions for AI & Big Data (SAB)
  • Cyber Physical Systems and Social Networks (CSN)
The goal of the RDS is to foster interaction and discussion through hands-on presentations that bring research to life. Unlike traditional oral or poster presentations, this session focuses on live demos.

Submission Guidelines:
Authors must submit a short paper (2–4 pages) in IEEE double-column conference format including:
  • Title, Authors, and Affiliations
  • Abstract
  • Introduction and Motivation
  • Proposed Design (including experimental results)
  • Conclusions and References
  • First author must be a student.
  • Submit a single PDF via the Microsoft CMT submission system under the "Research Demo Session" track.
  • Each proposal will be peer-reviewed.
  • Accepted papers will be published in the official IEEE conference proceedings (IEEE Xplore).

Additional Information:
  • A Best Research Demo award will be presented during the conference banquet.
  • Student first authors selected for the demo session will be eligible for travel grants.
  • We encourage submissions from both academia and industry that highlight innovative systems and compelling use cases.

For questions regarding the Research Demonstrations, please contact:
  • Dr. Deepak Bharti – deepak.ece@mnit.ac.in
  • Dr. Seema Aarella – saarella@austincollege.edu
