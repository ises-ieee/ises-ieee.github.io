Call for Pre-Conference Tutorials (PCT)


Call for PCT PDF: Download PDF

The IEEE International Symposium on Smart Electronic Systems (IEEE iSES 2025) invites proposals for Pre-Conference Tutorials that explore emerging trends and concepts in the design and applications of Smart Electronic Systems & VLSI.

We encourage tutorial proposals—both academic and industrial—that introduce novel topics or address recent technical trends in areas such as:
  • Home Automation
  • Wearables
  • Healthcare Systems
  • Smart Cities
  • Supply Chains
  • Smart Farming
  • Intelligent and Autonomous Systems
  • And others that are of interest to IEEE-iSES participant
These tutorials aim to provide insights into emerging problems or cutting-edge solutions relevant to IEEE iSES participants.

Schedule:
Pre-Conference Tutorials will be conducted two days prior of December 13 and 14, 2025. Each tutorial is expected to be 2–3 hours long.

Proposal Submission Guidelines:
Prepare a single PDF file (1–2 pages), using the template, that includes:
  • A description of the session topic, format, and intended audience
  • List of speakers with biographical sketches
  • Presentation titles and brief abstracts
  • Submit your tutorial proposal through the Microsoft CMT system.
  • The Microsoft CMT system will be used for reviewing the tutorials.
  • Detailed submission instructions will be provided upon proposal acceptance.

For questions regarding Pre-Conference Tutorials, please contact:
  • Dr. Rajesh Kedia – rkedia@cse.iith.ac.in
  • Dr. Sandeep Saini – sandeep.saini@lnmiit.ac.in
