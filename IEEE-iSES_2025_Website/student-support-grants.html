Student Support Grants

A limited number of Student Travel Support Grants are available for those attending IEEE iSES 2025.

Shortlisted candidates will be notified and must register for the conference by paying the eligible registration fee (student fee for RDS/SRF, full fee for paper if presenting, delegate fee if co-author). Participants with partial funding from their respective organizations will be given preference.

Please indicate the specific type of support you need: Travel, Accommodation, or Both. This helps us extend support to a larger number of participants.

For any queries, please email ieeeises2025@mnit.ac.in with “Support Grant” in the subject.

Please submit your applications ONLY through Microsoft CMT. Fellowship application for student grant: Application.
