How to Reach MNIT Jaipur

The Institute is located in Malaviya Nagar on Jawahar Lal Nehru Marg, Jaipur. Jaipur is situated in Northern India, approximately 260 km south of Delhi.

By Road

From National Highway 8

NH 8 connects Jaipur to Delhi, Ajmer, Udaipur, Ahmedabad, Vadodara, and Mumbai. Travelers can reach Sindhi Camp Bus Stand, which is about 10 km from MNIT. From there, they can take bus no. 7 or 17 to MNIT. Prepaid taxis are also available for a direct ride.

From National Highway 11

NH 11 connects Agra, Jaipur, and Bikaner. Travelers can reach Sindhi Camp Bus Stand, located 10 km from MNIT. From there, bus no. 7 or 17 can be taken to MNIT. Prepaid taxis are also available for direct transportation.

From National Highway 12

NH 12 connects Jabalpur, Bhopal, Khilchipur, Aklera, Jhalawar, Kota, Bundi, Devli, Tonk, and Jaipur. Travelers can reach Sindhi Camp Bus Stand, about 10 km from MNIT. From there, bus no. 7 or 17 provides direct connectivity. Prepaid taxis are also available.

By Train

Indian Railways connects Jaipur to almost all major cities in India. The three nearest railway stations to MNIT are:

Durgapura Railway Station

Located about 3 km from MNIT, travelers can hire a taxi for a direct ride.

Gandhi Nagar Railway Station

Located about 1 km from MNIT, travelers can either take a taxi or board bus no. 7 or 17 to reach the campus.

Jaipur Junction Railway Station

The main railway station in Jaipur is approximately 13 km from MNIT. Travelers can hire a taxi or take bus no. 7 for direct transport to MNIT.

By Air

MNIT is approximately 5 km from Sanganer Airport. Travelers can either hire a taxi for a direct ride or take bus no. 13 to Tonk Phatak and then transfer to bus no. 7 or 17 to reach MNIT.

Each mode of transportation provides a convenient way to reach the MNIT Jaipur campus from different locations.
